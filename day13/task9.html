Fluid Typography with em & rem

Watch how everything scales smoothly on any device.

The Power of rem

rem stands for root em — it scales text relative to the root font size (usually the html element). Change the base font size, and your whole page adjusts proportionally.

The Flexibility of em

em scales relative to the parent element’s font size. It’s great for modular scaling — headings, buttons, or cards that adapt to their container size.
