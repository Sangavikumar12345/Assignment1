STEPS FOR CHICKEN FRIED RICE

  • Prepare the chicken:
    1. Clean 1 whole chicken (about 1.2–1.5 kg).
    2. Rub it with salt to exfoliate and rinse thoroughly.
    3. Optionally stuff the cavity with slices of ginger and a few green onions.
  • Boil the chicken:
    1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
    2. Add a few slices of ginger and green onions.
    3. Gently lower the chicken into the pot and simmer for 30–40 minutes (until cooked through).
  • Cool the chicken:
    1. Remove the chicken and place it in an ice bath to stop the cooking and tighten the skin.
    2. Let it rest and then chop into serving pieces.
  • Make the chicken rice:
    1. Wash 2 cups of jasmine or long-grain rice.
    2. In a pan or rice cooker, sauté minced garlic, ginger, and shallots in chicken fat or oil until fragrant.
    3. Add the rice and stir for 2–3 minutes.
    4. transfer to a rice cooker, add 2 cups of the chicken broth from earlier, and cook until done.
  • Prepare the sauces:
    1. Chili sauce: Blend red chilies, garlic, ginger, lime juice, and chicken broth.
  • Make a simple soup (optional):
    1. Use the remaining chicken broth, season with salt and pepper.
    2. Add tofu, cabbage, or spring onions for a light side soup.
  • Assemble and serve:
    1. Plate the fragrant rice with sliced chicken.
    2. Drizzle with soy dressing and serve with sauces and soup on the side.
    3. Garnish with cucumber slices and cilantro.
